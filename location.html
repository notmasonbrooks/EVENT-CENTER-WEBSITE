How to find us!
  • Located on the Ridge of Mount Rhodesia, overlooking the valley, lies The Rhodesian. 20 miles south of Las Shegas, it is easy to find and hard to miss.
  • After about a half hour, get off of the interstate at exit 69.
  • Head east toward the town of Rhodesia and the take Hayward Road up the mountain.
