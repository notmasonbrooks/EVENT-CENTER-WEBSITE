Welcome to the Rhodesian

The Rhodesian is not just an event center. Here we pride ourselves in creating a one-of-a-kind experience where we curate a combination of world-class entertainment on our cliffside stage, enjoy a 5-star meal provided by our chefs from around the world, and stay in our all inclusive award winning resort, hang by the pool, and rest well in any number of our suites with canyon views.
